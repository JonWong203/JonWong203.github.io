What else?

The Sovereign Individual

A book predicting the triumph of the individual power over the nation-state in the 21st century. Written in late 1990s. Super forward-looking. Difficult but extremely interesting read.

Illuminates the realities of relationships. I either like people for who they are or I don't There is no need to change people or create an image of people.

Recommended by Ali Abdaal for everyone. Everyone should create to find their own voice.

Currently reading. Talks about why we cannot rely on fiat currency to maintain its value.
